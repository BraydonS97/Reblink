Rules

1. No hacking or glitch exploitation.
2. No blatant harrassment/racism in chat or discord.
3. Accounts with more than 2 VAC / GAMEBAN will not be able to join the server.
4. DDOS threats will result in a permanant ban.
5. Chat SPAM will result in a temporary mute.
6. Do not impersonate staff.
7. An association with a hacker will result with the same punishment the hacker had.
8. Have fun. If you witness a rule breaker use the /ticket command.

Commands

Home

/home "Name" - Teleports you home
/home add "Name" - Adds a new home
/home remove "Name" - Removes home
/home list - Shows list of all your homes

Teleport

/tpr "Player" - Sends teleport request.
/tpa - Accepts current teleport request.
/tpc - Cancels current teleport request.

Ticket

/ticket create "Issue" - To submit a ticket.
/ticket list - To list all your tickets.
/ticket view "Ticket ID" - To view your ticket.
/ticket reply "Ticket ID" "Msg" - To reply to your ticket.

Trades

/trade "Player" - To send a trade request
/trade accept - To accept the trade request
